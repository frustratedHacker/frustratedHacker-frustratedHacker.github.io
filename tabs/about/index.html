About

This is an initiative by a frustrated hacker to help people keen to learn about hacking.

Things written in the articles here won’t specify the good thing or bad thing. It will be on you how you use it. But we will not be resposible for any damage caused by you. We will strictly suggest to stick to white hat hacking and playing safe.

This is just the beginning of this initiative. Your suggestions will be highly appreciated. You can ask any kind of doubt or request to post any content.

We will cover everything including Web Security, Android Bug Hunting, Network Security, Forensics, Binary Exploitaion and alot more, both theoretically and practically.

Good luck :)
